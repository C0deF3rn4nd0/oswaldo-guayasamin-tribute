Oswaldo Guayasamin

The most amazing Ecuadorian Artist

Here is a resume of the life of an amazing artist

  • Oswaldo Aparicio Guayasamin was born on Quito on July 6th of 1919
  • He was an indigenous with kichwa ascendancy but his mother Dolres Calero was half-caste
  • His father was Jose Miguel Guayasamin and was a carpenter that later on worked as a taxi driver and truck driver
  • His artistic atitude woke up at an early age
  • When he was 8 years old he used to draw cartoons of his teachers and schoolmates
  • He used to do some drawings on pieces of canvas and cardboard, with landscapes and portraits of movie stars
  • Even that his father didn't approve he went to the Beautiful Arts School in Quito
  • He lived a really impactant during a civil war in the "Four day Battle" where his friend Manjarres died marking his art work later on his life inspiring "The Dead Kids" marking it's vision of humankind and society
  • He continues his studies and at 1941 he is granted with a diploma of painter and scuplptor as he had studied Architecture aswell
  • At the age of 23 in 1942 has it's first exposition on a particular studio in Quito being a scandal
  • Critics consider it's work as a confrontation with the official exposition of the Beautiful Arts School
  • Nelson Rockefeller impresed with his work buys various paints and helps Guaysamin in the future
  • Between 1942 and 1943 went six months to the United States and travels to Mexico where he meets the master Orozco that accepts to have Guayasamin as a assistant
  • He starts a friendship with Pablo Neruda and a year later travels through South America finding problems in common between this countries with an Opresed Indigenous society, finding there inspiration of it's most representative art-works
  • Later, on his pieces he tries to represent social themes trying to simplify it's shapes
  • With his amazing art he won National Prizes and international prizes at his 36 years old won the prize of the 3rd Bienal Hispanoamercan Art that had place on Barcelona, Spain and later on the Bienal at Sao Paulo
  • He was elected as the president of the Ecuadorian Cultural House in 1971
  • Had some expositions through the world in countires like:

    • Cuba

    • Spain

  • In 1976 he started the Guayasamin Foundation in Quito where he make the decition to donate it's artwork and starts the heritage of the people
  • In 1978 becomes a member of the Royal Academy of Beautiful Arts of Saint Fernando, Spain; a year later becomes member ot the Academy of Beautiful Arts of Italy
  • In 1982 he inaugurates the Barajas Airport with a mural of 120 meters painted by Guayasamin, that was splitted in two parts: one dedicated to Spain and the other to Latin America
  • October 28th, 1992 receips the title of Doctor Honoris Causa by the Faculty of Architecture and Arts of the Pedro Henriquez Ure√±a National University UNPHU
  • It's last expositions that he was capable to innaugurate were at the Luxembourg Palace Museum, Paris and the Palais de Glace Museum at Buenos Aires, Argentina in 1995
  • He exposed it's art pieces in all the museums of the capital cities at Hispanoamerica and many countries through Europe such as Saint Petesbourg, Moscow, Prague, Rome, Madrid, Barcelona and Varsovia
  • Made 48 individual expositions and his production was amazing with easel paintings, murals, sculptures and monuments
  • His art was marked as expressionist, it reflects the pain and misery that most of humanity endures and denounces the violence that human beings have experienced
  • Was friend of many important figures of the world and portrayed some of them like: Fidel and Raul Castro, Paco de Lucia, Francois and Danielle Mitterrand, Pablo Neruda, Gabriel Garcia Marquez, Rigoberta Menchu, Mercedes Sosa, King Juan Carlos of Spain and the Princess Carolina of Monaco, and many others
